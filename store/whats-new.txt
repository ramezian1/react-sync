v1.5.1

• Tab A and Tab B selections no longer reset — your picks now survive closing the popup, minimizing the browser, or switching to another tab. Previously you had to re-pick both dropdowns every time the popup closed (unless sync was already active).

• Auto-detect no longer hangs if a video pauses mid-capture — if either tab is paused during the 10-second audio capture, you now get a clear "keep both videos playing through the full 10s" message within ~13 seconds. Captures that complete at least 40% of the window still produce a result instead of erroring.
