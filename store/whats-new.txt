v1.5.0

• Mark sync point — new ⊙ A and ⊙ B buttons in the popup. Seek each tab to the same moment (e.g. Tab A to where the movie starts in the reaction, Tab B to 0:00), click both marks, and the offset is calculated and applied instantly. Works on all sites including Netflix and Disney+.

• Auto-detect offset fixed — was failing every time due to a browser audio policy issue. Now works correctly on YouTube, Patreon, Vimeo, Twitch, and other non-DRM sites. Both videos must be playing when you click auto-detect.

• Nudge buttons now sync instantly — clicking + or − while synced updates the background immediately, matching the keyboard shortcut behaviour. No need to re-click SYNC.

• Onboarding guide — a visual setup page now opens on first install explaining Tab A/B, the offset concept, and keyboard shortcuts.
