⟳ ReactSync Keyboard Shortcuts
Offset Nudge
Alt + Shift + ↑
Nudge offset +0.5s
A moves further ahead of B
Alt + Shift + ↓
Nudge offset −0.5s
B moves further ahead of A
Nudge shortcuts work from any tab — you don't need to open the popup. The offset adjusts live and B seeks immediately to the new position.

These shortcuts only take effect while a sync is active.
Customise Shortcuts
Chrome lets you remap these to any key combo you prefer.

Go to chrome://extensions/shortcuts (click to copy, then paste in the address bar) and look for ReactSync.
Tips
Fine-tuning: Use the popup nudge buttons (or shortcuts) while both videos are playing to dial in the offset by ear. Aim for the reaction video's audio to feel natural over the source.

Auto-detect: Hit ◎ auto-detect offset in the popup once both tabs are playing — it cross-correlates the audio and sets the offset automatically. Works on YouTube; blocked on DRM-protected sites (Netflix, Disney+).
